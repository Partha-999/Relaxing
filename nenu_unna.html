Nenu Unna Kada

Edi ayna nenu oppinchukunta, nenu chusukunta, uk how much I’ll fight/do for you…and life matter naa pranam antey assala odalanu…Nuv annav kada idi antha 1 yr baguntadi ani, I PROMISE lifelong baguntadi okka roju kuda prema taggadu enduku antey, ninnu nenu timepass laaga preminchatledu, every inch of my heart is filled with youuu ammuuu..adi eppatiki alane untadiii…Uncle laaga untanu emo ani bayapadaka…ee 2 yrs lo okkasari ayna anipinchinda nenu uncle laa unna ani?? Idi aarambam matrame, ninnu preminchadaniki or chusukodaniki ee process lo naaku alasata, visugu, ibbandi, baada ilantivi evi undavu I Promise…!!!

Nuv chaala manchi daanivi, naaku baa istam, nuv antey andariki istam untadi, andaru lo kondaru ninnu premistaru, aa kondaru lo chivari daaka nee aakari swaasa daaka nene ostha, nuv adagaale kaani nee chithi loki kuda osthaa, idi saradagano leda edo cheppali ani cheppatledu, manasu purthiga cheptuna ammuu, nuv antey praname….

Nuv lekunda nenu undalenu, neeku kastam ostey nenu tattukolenu, nee kastanni panchukoni solve cheddam ani unta, asala kastame raakudadu ani alochistha, ala chusukodaniki prathi adugu munduku vestha, nuv anandam ga undali anukunta, nee anandam lo nenu undali ani korukunta ammu, nuv antey praname, emina chestha nee kosam….

Nee meeda okkasari kopam raadu, antey nenu baristuna ani kaadu, asala barinche avakasam nuv ivvatledu, bariddam ani naa manasu kuda cheppatledu, ninnu premiddam, end daaka premistune undam ani naa manasu kuda cheptundi ammuuu….

Nee andanni preminchi poojinche avakasam istava, leda nee andaniki kapalaga unchuthava? Edina nee istame ammu, kaani nee vente nenu untaa…Ninnu premistunaaaaa….

Vaalla andarni oppinchadam naadi baadyatha ammu and nenu unna neeku, em ayina chusukunta namme chaalu.
